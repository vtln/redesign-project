About Us

Our Mission and Values

Active in Missions

2C3H is dedicated to active engagement in missions both locally and abroad, reaching out to those suffering spiritually, emotionally, and physically.

Programs and Support

We are running an ongoing feeding program in Kenya, Africa, and support a special needs home there with food supplies. In addition, we have donated wheelchairs to aid mobility-impaired children.

Meet Our Founder

Hi, my name is Patricia Rigsby-Scott and I'm a licensed and ordained minister and wife to my wonderful supportive husband, Cedric. He's my preparer, picker upper, and packer. He helps and supports me by letting me do what I do, and I appreciate him for that! I have always had the desire to help. Caring Compassionate Hearts & Helping Hands was birthed in 2009.
