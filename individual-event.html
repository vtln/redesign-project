Mobile Food Pantry

About this event

Friday, March 15th, 2024

12:00 PM to 4:00 PM

Waxahachie, TX

The primary goal of a mobile pantry is to address food insecurity by providing access to nutritious food for individuals and families facing financial challenges or living in underserved areas. By bringing food directly to communities, mobile pantries help overcome barriers such as transportation issues, lack of nearby grocery stores, or limited resources.
