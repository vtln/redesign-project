Food Assistance

About this program

Year-round program

Supports about 10-15 people

Our food assistance program is dedicated to providing vital support to individuals and families who are facing food insecurity. Through this program, we offer access to nutritious meals, essential groceries, and food resources to ensure that no one in our community goes hungry. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where individuals can access the food they need with dignity and respect. We are committed to serving our community with compassion, empathy, and a dedication to making a positive difference in the lives of those we assist.

Goals for this Program

Alleviate Hunger

We aim to ensure that everyone in our community has access to nutritious meals, addressing the urgent need for food support.

Promote Health & Well-Being

By providing access to healthy meals, we strive to improve overall health outcomes and support individuals in leading healthier lives.

Foster Community Support

By bringing people together to address the fundamental need for food, we build stronger community connections and mutual support networks, creating a more resilient and supportive community.
